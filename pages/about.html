About Me

I am a Ph.D. scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, supervised by Dr. Rameswar Pratap .

My current research focuses on designing efficient and accurate sketching algorithms for high-dimensional multimodal tensor data. I work on randomized methods for dimensionality reduction and similarity estimation, aiming to make large-scale machine learning faster and more scalable while preserving important information in the data.
